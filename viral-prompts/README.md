# Viral Prompts

A curated collection of viral ChatGPT (GPT-4o) image prompts by [@techwithgul](https://www.instagram.com/techwithgul.ai) — the exact, copy-paste-ready prompts behind the most-shared AI photo transformations of 2026.

**How every prompt works:**
1. Open ChatGPT (GPT-4o required for image gen) — https://chat.openai.com
2. Upload your photo
3. Paste the prompt
4. Generate

---

## The Prompts — Ranked Best to Worst

> Ranking based on shareability, save rate, and how reliably the result lands.

### #1 — Met Gala Red Carpet 🏆

> **Why it wins:** Changes context entirely — you're *placed*, not edited. Peaked May 2026.

```
Transform me into a Met Gala celebrity on the red carpet.
Luxury haute couture outfit, dramatic editorial lighting,
Vogue magazine cover composition, paparazzi flash photography,
red carpet backdrop, ultra-glamorous, cinematic quality.
Pose: confident, commanding, high-fashion.
```

---

### #2 — Studio Ghibli Anime

> **Why #2:** Highest save rate. People use the result as wallpaper + profile pic.

```
Reimagine this photo in Studio Ghibli anime art style.
Soft watercolor backgrounds, lush greenery, golden hour
lighting, hand-painted texture, whimsical and peaceful mood.
Keep my face recognizable but stylized in Ghibli character form.
```

---

### #3 — Pixar 3D Transform

> **Why #3:** Works on any photo, any lighting. Hard to get wrong.
> The two words that make it work: *"keep my facial features recognizable"* (without it, you get a generic character, not you) and *"modern tech workspace"* (sets the whole scene in four words).

```
Transform this photo into a Pixar-style 3D animated character.
Keep my facial features recognizable. Studio lighting,
warm colors, cinematic Pixar quality. Character sitting
confidently in a modern tech workspace with subtle glowing
screens in the background.
```

---

### #4 — Hollywood Movie Poster

> **Why #4:** Great results but requires a specific backstory in the prompt.

```
Turn this into a cinematic movie poster. I am the main
character — a tech genius hacker. Dark moody lighting,
dramatic title typography at bottom, Hollywood blockbuster
poster style, lens flare, epic composition.
```

### #5 — Y2K Flash Party Photo

> **Why #5:** Fun and shareable but oversaturated — everyone's doing it.

```
Edit this photo to look like it was taken on a 2000s digital
camera at a party. Direct camera flash, high contrast,
glossy skin, overexposed highlights, grainy texture,
slightly washed-out colors, casual candid framing.
Y2K aesthetic. Instagram flash filter style.
```

---

### #6 — Neo-Noir Cyberpunk

> **Why #6:** Cinematic result, but quality depends heavily on the original photo's lighting.

```
Transform this photo into a cinematic neo-noir portrait.
Deep blue and magenta neon lighting, rain streaks on glass
behind me, wet streets reflecting neon signs, dramatic
shadows, ultra-sharp cinematic photography, 8K quality,
moody cyberpunk atmosphere. Keep my face realistic.
```

## Tips for Better Results

- **Keep your face:** Always include a line like *"keep my facial features recognizable"* — without it, AI drifts toward a generic stranger.
- **Set a scene, not just a style:** A few words of context ("modern tech workspace", "red carpet backdrop") give the image a story.
- **Use a clear source photo:** Good, even lighting in your original = dramatically better output, especially for cinematic styles (#1, #4, #6).
- **One subject works best:** Solo portraits transform more cleanly than group shots.
